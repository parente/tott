    block body

Example: Extends

// in login.jade
extends layout.jade

block head
  title Login
  link(rel="stylesheet", href="css/login.css")
  
block body
  form.login
    input#username
    input#password
    button(type="submit") Login
    
block postscript
  script(src="js/login.js")

A Word of Caution

Like everything else, templates have their place.

Don't write your whole app in a template!
